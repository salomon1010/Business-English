Delete your account & data

BE Mastery — Business English Mastery (12-Week System) · by Lomonec LLC

BE Mastery works without an account — everything runs on your device. An account only exists if you chose to sign in (with email or Google) to sync your progress across devices. This page explains how to delete that account and its data.

1. Delete your account & all synced data (in the app)

  1. Open BE Mastery and go to Account (tap ⋯ More → Account on phones).
  2. In the “☁️ Synced & signed in” card, tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and all data synced to it are permanently deleted immediately.

2. Or request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email contact@lomonec.com from the address you signed up with and ask us to delete your account. We will delete it within 7 days and confirm by reply.

3. Delete your data without deleting your account

In Account → Reset everything, you can erase all of your progress and recordings. If you are signed in, this also clears the progress synced to your account, while keeping the account itself.

What is deleted

  • Deleted permanently: your account (email), your profile name, and all synced progress — scores, streaks, vocabulary, and review notes stored in our Firebase/Firestore backend.
  • Stays only on your device: your audio recordings are never uploaded to us — they live on your device and are removed when you use “Reset everything” or uninstall the app.
  • Retention: account and cloud data are removed immediately on request; any residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days.
